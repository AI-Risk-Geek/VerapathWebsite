This is Verapath

Verapath was built to solve a growing problem: organizations want the power of AI, but cannot afford the regulatory, security, and reputational risks that come with uncontrolled adoption.

We provide a third path. Not public AI tools you don’t control, and not costly in-house platforms that take years to build. Verapath is a private AI governance platform that runs in your environment, enforces your policies, and produces evidence regulators can trust.

Our customers use Verapath to safely deploy AI across operations, research, and client-facing workflows— without exposing sensitive data or creating audit risk.
